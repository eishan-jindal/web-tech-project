Ullas

"The greatest joy in life is the joy of giving to others" : PROJECT ULLAS

The greatest joy in life is the joy of giving to others. A staunch follower of this saying FEPSI came up with Ullas.Ullas is an unprecedented campaign being led by Fepsi which focuses on encouraging people to spread love and share their happiness with the less fortunate. We set out officially with this idea on 25th October, 2013, the birthday of GV Selvam Sir under the guidance of Prof. S Tamil Maran at Brahmapuram Secondary High School, Vellore. Ullas has only grown since then. We believe that our special days such as our birthdays or festivals, should be shared with people who only dream of happiness. Bringing a smile on other people's face is what we always strive for. We believe in spreading love. We dream to share our happiness with such people and bring a change in their lives, in any way possible. This belief resonates with Albert Einstein words: Happiness is only real when shared. Apart from this, we take out time to conduct outreaches once every week. We visit orphanages or old age homes and spend time there. We learn from their problems and sufferings and try to give them at least a day full of memories to be cherished forever. After all, Goodness Boomerangs!

Soch

"A youth without fire is followed by an old age without experience." : PROJECT SOCH


"A youth without fire is followed by an old age without experience." Fep-Si through its Project Soch - a one of its kind endeavor to bring together motivated and inspired young adults from across the world to deliberate and work towards effective Policy, Change and Development - aims to provide an avenue for free speech and mass recognition of open conversations. It is our 'Call for Action' to the youth worldwide. Through this initiative we wish to provide a positive stimulus to individuals - the youth - who want to make a difference. We aim to connect various Student Groups, Universities, Colleges, NGO's, Social Organizations and Youth Communities to work together for a better tomorrow by making their voices count.
